About

Jahangirnagar University Debate Organization, popularly recognized as JUDO, is the central debate entity of Jahangirnagar University. It was established on December 23, 2005. It has been operating its activities with great accomplishments and excellence of its dedicated members since then. More than 150 running students are currently working with JUDO. JUDO regulates the debate workouts of the departments and halls of Jahangirnagar University having 12 officially affiliated debating clubs. JUDO organizes National Debate Festival every year with three mandatory segments of inter-school, inter-college and inter-university parliamentary debate competition with the participation of approximately 300 debaters which is said to be one of the biggest debate events in Bangladesh. Alongside, JUDO organizes national Barwary debate competitions, national public speaking competitions, so many thematic debate events & various internal debate events like - Inter-department debate competition, Inter-hall debate competition, Freshers' reception with debate workshop, Freshers' debate competition etc. JUDO acknowledges in nourishing new debaters with the utmost care. It also focuses on impacting socio-cultural outcomes directly through debating. With the visionary dream and the great dedication of the members, JUDO has become one of the prominent debate organizations in Bangladesh. Its bunch of achievements and numerous contributions to the debate movement of the country made it the most thriving debate entity of the territory.
